Overview

Timely is a time management application that allows you to create, manage, organize, and track the progress of your projects. Get started by simply creating a project and specify your tasks. When you're ready to start working on a task, navigate to the task you're working on and click Start. Done working for the day or simply taking a break? No problem! Click Pause in your task view to punch your time card.

How to:

Create a project

To create a project, click on the create a project button on the Projects page and enter the project nane, your first task name, and notes for that task

View more info about your project

After you have created a project, you will see your project show up on your Projects page. to gain more info, click on the name of the project, and you will be taken to a page showing you your list of tasks for the project, which tasks are completed, the percentage of your project that's completed, as well as other tools to manage the project such as editing the project name.

Create additional tasks

You can create additional tasks for your project to manage by clicking on the 'Add Task' button on your project overview page. This will take you to a screen allowing you to enter info regarding the new task name and notes for the new task.

View more info about your tasks

Similarly with getting more info about your project, you can click on the name of the task you want more info about on your project overview page, and you will be taken to a task overview page where you wil see your notes and tools to manage your task. You can even edit your task name and mark it as complete! If you mark your task as complete, you will see the change reflected on your project overview page

Starting a task

Starting a task will begin keeping track of your time working on the task. When you're done working on the task for the day, just click 'Stop working on task' button and your time spent on task will update. NOTE, it is important to make sure to end your task. If you haven't been working on the task for at least a minute, your task time will not update

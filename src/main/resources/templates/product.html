Overview

Timely is a time management application that allows you to create, manage, organize, and track the progress of your projects. Get started by simply creating a project and specify your tasks. When you're ready to start working on a task, navigate to the task you're working on and click Start. Done working for the day or simply taking a break? No problem! Click Stop in your task view to punch your time card.

How to:

Create a project

Create your first project once you sign up! To create additional projects, click on the create a project button on the Projects page and enter the project name and the name of your first task.

View more info about your project

After you have created a project, you will see your project show up on your Projects page. To gain more info click on the name of the project, and you will be taken to a page showing you your list of tasks for the project. Each task has information such as the status of task and how much time has been spent on the task. Click on the name of the task to get access to your notes!

Starting a task will begin keeping track of your time working on the task and update the status to "In progress". When you're done working on the task for the day, just click 'Stop' button and your time spent on task will update.

Create additional tasks

You can create additional tasks for your project to manage by clicking on the 'Create task' button on your project overview page. This will take you to a screen allowing you to enter info regarding the new task name, status, and notes for the new task.
